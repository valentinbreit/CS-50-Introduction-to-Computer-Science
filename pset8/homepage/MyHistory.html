A little Story of Valentin Breit...

When I was born, my family was really small and falled apart, beacuse both of my parents were really young and my family wasn´t able to believe that they could be a great and wonderful marriage and raise a son with that age. This was due to the fact that in my country these kind of situations happen very often and it ends with a young pregnant lady and a son born and raised without a father figure. Years later in my walk through kinder garden, primary and high school my family solved their problems and see both, my parents and I growing up to be a young adults. Besides that, I was like my mother always told me " An old man soul trapped in a young man body" and that never helped me to do friends neither in kinder or primary school. I remember my step foward highschool, learning a lot of things, for example my first job or my International Cheff course. Nowadays I´m studying not only this course in cs50 but also Information and Communication Technologies Analyst in the Universidad Nacional de La Plata looking foward to being a great professional.
